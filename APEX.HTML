Artificial Intelligence & Data Science



What is Artificial Intelligence?


1. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines.
2. AI enables computers to perform tasks that typically require human cognition, such as learning and problem-solving.
3. It includes subfields like machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing.
4. AI systems can analyze large amounts of data and make decisions based on patterns.
5. It is used in various applications, including robotics, healthcare, finance, and customer service.
6. AI can be categorized into Narrow AI (task-specific) and General AI (human-like intelligence).
7. Technologies like neural networks and reinforcement learning improve AI performance.
8. AI-powered chatbots, virtual assistants, and self-driving cars are real-world examples.
9. Ethical concerns include data privacy, job displacement, and decision-making biases.
10. AI continues to evolve, shaping the future of technology and human interactions.







What is Data Science?


1.Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that extracts insights and knowledge from structured and unstructured data.
2. It combines statistics, mathematics, programming, and domain expertise to analyze and interpret data.
3. Machine learning and artificial intelligence are key components used in data science.
4. Data scientists work with large datasets using tools like Python, R, SQL, and cloud computing.
5. The process involves data collection, cleaning, analysis, visualization, and decision-making.
6. It is widely used in healthcare, finance, marketing, e-commerce, and various industries.
7. Data science helps in predictive analytics, recommendation systems, and automation.
8. Common techniques include regression, classification, clustering, and deep learning.
9. Ethical concerns include data privacy, bias in models, and responsible AI usage.
10. The demand for data scientists is growing as businesses rely more on data-driven decisions.
